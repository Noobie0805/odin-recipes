Apple Pie

Description :

An apple pie is a pie in which the principal filling ingredient is apple. It is often served with whipped cream, ice cream, or cheddar cheese. It is generally double-crusted, with pastry both above and below the filling; the upper crust may be solid or latticed (woven of crosswise strips). A traditional British apple pie has a bottom crust, but not a top crust; this is a "tart". The English style of apple pie recipe usually calls for a shortcrust pastry, as opposed to the American style of pie, which calls for a flaky pastry. The apple pie is a traditional dish in the United Kingdom, United States, Canada, and Sweden.

Ingredients

  • 1 package of double-crust pie dough (or homemade crust)
  • 6-7 medium apples (Granny Smith or Honeycrisp recommended)
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter (cut into small pieces)
  • 1 egg (beaten, for egg wash)
  • 1 tablespoon coarse sugar (optional, for topping)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Peel, core, and slice the apples into 1/4-inch thick slices. Place them in a large mixing bowl.
  3. Add 1 tablespoon of lemon juice, 1/2 cup granulated sugar, 1/4 cup brown sugar, 1 teaspoon cinnamon, 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg, 1/4 teaspoon salt, and 2 tablespoons flour to the apples. Toss to combine evenly.
  4. Roll out one of the pie doughs and fit it into a 9-inch pie dish.
  5. Pour the apple mixture into the pie crust, arranging it evenly. Dot the filling with small pieces of butter.
  6. Roll out the second pie dough and place it over the apples. Trim any excess dough and crimp the edges together to seal the pie.
  7. Brush the top crust with the beaten egg and sprinkle with coarse sugar, if desired. Cut a few small slits in the top crust to allow steam to escape during baking.
  8. Bake the pie in the preheated oven for 45-50 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the filling is bubbling.
  9. If the edges of the crust brown too quickly, cover them with aluminum foil halfway through the baking process.
  10. Remove the pie from the oven and let it cool for at least 2 hours before slicing and serving.
Enjoy your apple pie!
